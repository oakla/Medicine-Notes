**three types of hormones**
1. protein/petide
2. tyrosine-derived 
3. steroid
Remember the steroids and the tyrosine derivatives, everything else is a protein/peptide
- **Tyrosine Derivatives**:
	- Epinephrine
	- Norepinepherine
	- Dopamine
	- Triiodothyronine
	- Thyroxine

	- Cortisol
	- Aldosterone
	- Vitamin D

	- Thyrotropin-releasing hormone
